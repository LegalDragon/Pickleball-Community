# Video Script: Managing Your Registration & Payment

**Duration:** ~2.5 minutes
**Target Audience:** Registered users who need to manage their registration or pay

---

## Introduction (10 seconds)

**Narration:**
"Once you're registered, here's how to manage your registration, complete payment, and cancel if needed."

---

## Step 1: Finding Your Registrations (15 seconds)

**Action:** Go to Events page, My Events tab

**Screenshot 1:** Events page with "My Events" tab selected
**Screenshot 2:** "Registered" sub-tab showing your events

**Narration:**
"Go to the Events page and select 'My Events'. The 'Registered' tab shows all events you've signed up for."

---

## Step 2: Open Event Details (10 seconds)

**Action:** Click on a registered event

**Screenshot:** Event card with your registration indicator

**Narration:**
"Click on any event to open its details. You'll see you're registered right away."

---

## Step 3: View Your Registration Details (20 seconds)

**Action:** Show the green "View My Registrations" button, then click it

**Screenshot 1:** Green "View My Registrations" button on Details tab
**Screenshot 2:** Registrations tab with "Your Registrations" section expanded

**Narration:**
"Click 'View My Registrations' to go to the Registrations tab. At the top, you'll see all your registrations for this event - the division, your partner (if doubles), and payment status."

---

## Step 4: Understanding Payment Status (25 seconds)

**Action:** Point to payment status indicators

**Screenshot 1:** "Pay" button (orange) - payment needed
**Screenshot 2:** "Pending" badge (yellow) - awaiting verification
**Screenshot 3:** "Paid" badge (green) - payment complete

**Narration:**
"Your registration card shows payment status:
- **Orange 'Pay' button** - You need to make a payment
- **Yellow 'Pending'** - You've submitted payment, waiting for organizer to verify
- **Green 'Paid'** - Payment is complete

Note: For incomplete pairs, payment may be disabled until your partner joins."

---

## Step 5: Making a Payment (30 seconds)

**Action:** Click Pay button and show payment modal

**Screenshot 1:** Clicking Pay button
**Screenshot 2:** Payment modal showing amount due
**Screenshot 3:** Payment options (online, manual instruction)
**Screenshot 4:** Payment confirmation

**Narration:**
"Click 'Pay' to open the payment window. You'll see:
- The total amount due
- Payment options set by the organizer

If online payment is available, enter your payment details and submit. If it's manual payment (like Venmo or cash), follow the organizer's instructions and mark your payment as submitted."

---

## Step 6: Payment Confirmation (15 seconds)

**Action:** Show payment submitted successfully

**Screenshot 1:** Success message
**Screenshot 2:** Status changed to "Pending" or "Paid"

**Narration:**
"After payment, you'll see a confirmation. If manual, it shows as 'Pending' until the organizer verifies. Online payments usually confirm immediately."

---

## Step 7: Finding a Partner (for incomplete pairs) (20 seconds)

**Action:** Show the "Find Partner" option on an incomplete registration

**Screenshot 1:** Registration showing "Looking for partner" status
**Screenshot 2:** "Find Partner" link
**Screenshot 3:** List of available partners

**Narration:**
"If you registered without a partner, your card shows 'Looking for partner'. Click 'Find Partner' to see others in the same situation. Click 'Join' next to anyone you'd like to pair with."

---

## Step 8: Canceling Your Registration (20 seconds)

**Action:** Click Cancel button

**Screenshot 1:** Cancel button on registration card
**Screenshot 2:** Confirmation dialog
**Screenshot 3:** Success message

**Narration:**
"Need to cancel? Click the red 'Cancel' button on your registration card. Confirm your choice.

**Important:** Cancellation policies vary by event. Some may not offer refunds after a certain date, so check the event details."

---

## Step 9: Viewing Registration History (15 seconds)

**Action:** Show past events in My Events

**Screenshot:** My Events showing past registered events

**Narration:**
"Your past registrations stay in 'My Events' for your records, even after the event ends."

---

## Closing (10 seconds)

**Narration:**
"That's everything you need to manage your registrations. Good luck and have fun playing!"
