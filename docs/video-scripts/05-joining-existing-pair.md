# Video Script: Joining an Existing Pair (Finding a Partner)

**Duration:** ~2 minutes
**Target Audience:** Users without a partner who want to join someone else's team

---

## Introduction (10 seconds)

**Narration:**
"Don't have a partner? No problem! You can join someone who's already registered and looking for a teammate."

---

## Step 1: Find a Division with Players Looking (15 seconds)

**Action:** Go to Registrations tab and select a doubles division

**Screenshot:** Division dropdown or division cards showing "X players looking for a partner"

**Narration:**
"In the Registrations tab, look for divisions that show 'players looking for a partner'. This means there are incomplete pairs you can join."

---

## Step 2: View the "Join Existing Pair" Option (20 seconds)

**Action:** Select the division and scroll to see the join option

**Screenshot 1:** Division selected showing registered pairs
**Screenshot 2:** Blue "Join an existing pair" button at bottom

**Narration:**
"After selecting a division, scroll down. If there are players looking for partners, you'll see a blue 'Join an existing pair' button showing how many people are looking."

---

## Step 3: Click to See Available Partners (15 seconds)

**Action:** Click the "Join an existing pair" button

**Screenshot:** List of players/pairs looking for partners with their names and photos

**Narration:**
"Click the button to see a list of everyone looking for a partner in this division. You can see their name and profile photo."

---

## Step 4: View Player Profiles (15 seconds)

**Action:** Click on a player's name to view their profile

**Screenshot:** Player profile popup showing skill level, play style, etc.

**Narration:**
"Click on a player's name to view their profile. Check their skill level and play style to see if you'd be a good match."

---

## Step 5: Send Join Request (20 seconds)

**Action:** Click "Join" button next to the player you want to partner with

**Screenshot 1:** Join button next to player
**Screenshot 2:** Confirmation dialog
**Screenshot 3:** Success message "Request sent!"

**Narration:**
"Found a good match? Click the 'Join' button next to their name. Confirm your request, and they'll be notified that you want to partner with them."

---

## Step 6: Pending Request Status (20 seconds)

**Action:** Show the pending request in your registrations

**Screenshot 1:** "Pending Partner Requests" section in event details
**Screenshot 2:** Request showing "Waiting for [name] to accept"

**Narration:**
"Your request appears in the 'Pending Partner Requests' section. You'll see who you've requested to join and that you're waiting for them to accept."

---

## Step 7: Partner Accepts Your Request (15 seconds)

**Action:** Show notification when accepted

**Screenshot 1:** Notification "Your join request was accepted!"
**Screenshot 2:** Complete pair showing in Your Registrations

**Narration:**
"When they accept, you'll get a notification. Your registration is now complete and shows in 'Your Registrations' with both names."

---

## Step 8: If They Decline (10 seconds)

**Action:** Show declined request

**Screenshot:** Notification showing request was declined

**Narration:**
"If they decline, no worries - you can send a request to another player looking for a partner, or register solo and wait for someone to join you."

---

## Alternative: Register Solo First (15 seconds)

**Action:** Show registering solo option

**Screenshot:** Registration with "Looking for partner" option selected

**Narration:**
"Alternatively, you can register first and mark yourself as 'looking for a partner'. Others will then see you in the list and can request to join you."

---

## Closing (10 seconds)

**Narration:**
"That's how you find a partner! The system makes it easy to connect with other players looking for teammates."
