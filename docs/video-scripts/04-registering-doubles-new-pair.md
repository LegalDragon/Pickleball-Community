# Video Script: Registering for Doubles (Creating a New Pair)

**Duration:** ~2.5 minutes
**Target Audience:** Users registering for doubles with a partner in mind

---

## Introduction (10 seconds)

**Narration:**
"In this video, you'll learn how to register for a doubles division and invite a partner to join your team."

---

## Step 1: Navigate to Registrations Tab (10 seconds)

**Action:** From event details, click "Register Now"

**Screenshot:** Register Now button

**Narration:**
"From the event details, click 'Register Now' to go to the Registrations tab."

---

## Step 2: Select a Doubles Division (15 seconds)

**Action:** Select a doubles division from the dropdown

**Screenshot 1:** Division dropdown showing doubles divisions
**Screenshot 2:** Doubles division selected

**Narration:**
"Select a doubles division from the dropdown. These are marked as 'Doubles' or 'Pairs' format. Choose the one matching your skill level."

---

## Step 3: Click Register (15 seconds)

**Action:** Click the Register button

**Screenshot:** Register button for doubles division

**Narration:**
"Click 'Register' to begin. Since this is doubles, you'll need to either invite a partner or indicate you're looking for one."

---

## Step 4: Registration Options (20 seconds)

**Action:** Show the registration dialog/options

**Screenshot:** Registration dialog showing options: "I have a partner" vs "Looking for partner"

**Narration:**
"You have two options:
- **I have a partner** - Enter their email to send an invite
- **Looking for a partner** - Register solo and let others join you

Let's go through the 'I have a partner' flow first."

---

## Step 5: Invite Your Partner (25 seconds)

**Action:** Enter partner's email address

**Screenshot 1:** Partner email input field
**Screenshot 2:** Partner search results (if they're already a user)
**Screenshot 3:** Send invite button

**Narration:**
"If your partner already has an account, start typing their name or email - the system will show matching users. Select your partner from the list.

If they don't have an account yet, enter their email address and they'll receive an invitation to join."

---

## Step 6: Confirm Registration (15 seconds)

**Action:** Click confirm/submit

**Screenshot:** Confirmation of registration with pending partner status

**Narration:**
"Click to confirm. You're now registered, and your partner will receive an invitation to accept."

---

## Step 7: Registration Status - Pending Partner (20 seconds)

**Action:** Show the registration with pending partner

**Screenshot 1:** Your Registrations section showing pair with "(pending)" status
**Screenshot 2:** The pair in the division list showing "Looking" badge

**Narration:**
"Your registration shows in 'Your Registrations' with your partner marked as 'pending' until they accept. In the division list, your pair shows a 'Looking' badge since the team isn't complete yet."

---

## Step 8: What Your Partner Sees (20 seconds)

**Action:** Show notification/invite from partner's perspective

**Screenshot 1:** Email notification to partner
**Screenshot 2:** Partner's view of the pending invite in the app

**Narration:**
"Your partner receives an email notification. When they log in, they'll see the invite in their notifications and can accept or decline."

---

## Step 9: Partner Accepts (15 seconds)

**Action:** Show the completed pair after acceptance

**Screenshot 1:** Success notification "Partner joined!"
**Screenshot 2:** Pair now showing green "Complete" status

**Narration:**
"Once your partner accepts, your pair is complete! The status changes to green 'Complete' and you're all set for the tournament."

---

## Closing (10 seconds)

**Narration:**
"That's how you register with a partner. In the next video, we'll show you how to register solo and find a partner later."

---
