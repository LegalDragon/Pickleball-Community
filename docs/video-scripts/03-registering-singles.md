# Video Script: Registering for a Singles Division

**Duration:** ~1.5 minutes
**Target Audience:** Users registering for singles events

---

## Introduction (10 seconds)

**Narration:**
"Registering for a singles division is quick and easy. Let's walk through it."

---

## Prerequisites (10 seconds)

**Screenshot:** Login prompt or logged-in user avatar

**Narration:**
"Before registering, make sure you're logged in and your profile has your correct name and gender - these are used for your registration."

---

## Step 1: Navigate to Registrations Tab (10 seconds)

**Action:** From event details, click "Register Now" or "Registrations" tab

**Screenshot:** Register Now button being clicked

**Narration:**
"From the event details, click 'Register Now' to go to the Registrations tab."

---

## Step 2: Select Your Division (15 seconds)

**Action:** Click the division dropdown and select a singles division

**Screenshot 1:** Division dropdown open showing singles divisions
**Screenshot 2:** Singles division selected

**Narration:**
"Use the dropdown to select your division. Look for divisions marked as 'Singles' format. Select the one matching your skill level and any other criteria."

---

## Step 3: Click Register (15 seconds)

**Action:** Click the Register button for the selected division

**Screenshot:** Division detail showing Register button

**Narration:**
"Once you've selected your division, click the orange 'Register' button. If the division is full, you'll see 'Join Waitlist' instead."

---

## Step 4: Registration Confirmed (20 seconds)

**Action:** Show the success message and updated UI

**Screenshot 1:** Success toast message "Successfully registered!"
**Screenshot 2:** Green "Registered" badge now showing for that division
**Screenshot 3:** Your registration appearing in the list

**Narration:**
"You'll see a success message confirming your registration. The button changes to a green 'Registered' badge, and your name appears in the registered players list."

---

## Step 5: View Your Registration (15 seconds)

**Action:** Show the "Your Registrations" section at top of Registrations tab

**Screenshot:** Your Registrations section showing the singles registration

**Narration:**
"Your registration now appears in the 'Your Registrations' section at the top. From here you can manage your registration or make a payment if required."

---

## Step 6: Payment (if required) (15 seconds)

**Action:** Click the Pay button if there's a fee

**Screenshot:** Pay button on registration card

**Narration:**
"If there's an entry fee, click 'Pay' to complete your payment. The organizer may accept online payment or provide instructions for other payment methods."

---

## Closing (10 seconds)

**Narration:**
"That's it! You're registered for a singles event. Check your email for confirmation, and good luck in the tournament!"

---
